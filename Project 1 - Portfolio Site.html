Catwoman Comic Book Reviews

Catwoman has long been a favorite character of mine, going all the way back to Batman Returns, Batman: The Animated Series, and the first Catwoman ongoing series by writers like Jo Duffy and Chuck Dixon with that classic Jim Balent artwork. This current series hasn't been great but anytime I'm buying comics and there's a Catwoman series, I'll be buying it!

Red Hood Comic Book Reviews

Jason Todd, the second Robin, killed by the Joker and resurrected by Ra's ah Ghul, reborn as Red Hood... Jason is, to me, the most interesting of any of the characters that have held the title of 'Robin' as his rage at Batman's failure to avenge his death and his willingness, even proclivity, to kill criminals puts him in stark contrast with Gotham's Dark Knight. Red Hood: Outlaw is a fantastic series and you shoudl definetly add it to your reading list!
